Welcome to Sri Lanka. Explore Colombo’s colonial charm, vibrant markets, and seaside promenade.

Detailed Schedule

Highlights: Manawari Temple (Ramayana Trail Site) Sigiriya Rock Fortress (UNESCO)

Morning

Your spiritual journey begins with a pick-up from the Airport or Negombo. We'll start the day with a complimentary welcome breakfast in Negombo before heading to Manawari Temple in Chilaw. This is a significant site on the Ramayana trail, believed to be where Lord Rama first installed a Shiva Lingam to worship Lord Shiva after defeating King Ravana. The temple, with its profound history and spiritual ambiance, is an essential pilgrimage for devotees.

A Note on the Manawari Temple Visit:

A puja is a wonderful opportunity to immerse yourself in local culture and religious practices. We would like to confirm if you are willing to attend the puja during our visit. Please let us know so we can make the necessary arrangements.

Afternoon

Next, we'll travel to the awe-inspiring Sigiriya Rock Fortress. This UNESCO World Heritage site is a marvel of ancient engineering and art. While famous for its breathtaking frescoes and lion's paw entrance, Sigiriya also has a legendary connection to the Ramayana. It's believed that this rock fortress was once the pleasure palace of King Ravana, and its summit may have been where Sita was held captive. The panoramic views from the top are truly unforgettable.

Preparing for Your Sigiriya Visit:
  • Wear a hat with a chin strap or a cap you can secure.
  • Comfortable walking shoes with good grip.
  • Bring a light jacket or windbreaker.
  • Stay hydrated; carry a water bottle.
  • Keep belongings secure, especially near edges.

Evening

After exploring Sigiriya, we'll check into your hotel in Dambulla for a well-deserved rest and a delicious dinner, preparing you for the next day's adventures.

Highlights: Thirukoneswaram Kovil Kanniya Hot Springs

Morning

Following a hearty breakfast, your adventure continues with a scenic journey from Dambulla to the coastal city of Trincomalee. Our primary destination is the revered Thirukoneswaram Kovil, an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, dramatically situated atop a cliff. This sacred site holds significant importance in the Ramayana, believed to be where King Ravana performed penance to appease Lord Shiva. We'll explore the temple's rich history and serene atmosphere, and you'll have the option to attend a puja, offering a profound spiritual experience.

A Note on Temple Etiquette:

As Thirukoneswaram Kovil is an active place of worship, please dress respectfully, ensuring shoulders and knees are covered. You will also be required to remove footwear before entering the temple grounds.

Afternoon

Next, we'll visit the mystical Kanniya Hot Springs. These seven wells are famous for their unique temperature variations and are believed to have been created by Ravana's anger-fueled trident strike into the ground. The hot springs are a fascinating blend of myth and natural wonder, offering a chance to relax and reflect. You can dip your feet in the warm, therapeutic waters before we begin our journey back to Dambulla.

Preparing for Your Kanniya Hot Springs Visit:
  • Bring a small hand towel.
  • Wear comfortable footwear that’s easy to remove.

Evening

We'll return to your hotel in Dambulla, where you can unwind and enjoy a well-deserved dinner, reflecting on the day's spiritual and historical discoveries.

Highlights: Shri Bhakta Hanuman Temple (Ramayana Trail Site) Tea Plantation & Ramboda Falls

Morning

Your journey continues to the picturesque hill country, home to some of the most significant sites on the Ramayana trail. Our first stop is the Shri Bhakta Hanuman Temple in Ramboda. This sacred temple is a powerful tribute to Lord Hanuman, believed to be the very location where he began his search for Sita in the mystical land of Lanka. It is also historically significant as the place where Lord Rama's army, with its vast forces, was said to have gathered. The temple, maintained by the Chinmaya Mission of Sri Lanka, offers a tranquil and spiritual atmosphere amidst stunning mountain views.

Afternoon

After our visit, you will have a lunch break in Ramboda. Please note that lunch is not included in the tour package. Following lunch, we will enjoy a complimentary visit to a lush tea plantation where you can witness the tea-making process from leaf to cup. We will also make a stop at the magnificent Ramboda Falls for a breathtaking photo opportunity, a sight that will surely leave you in awe.

A Note on Lunch & Travel Options:

Should you prefer, we can also arrange for a late lunch with a quick shopping stop in the bustling city of Kandy, before continuing our journey to Nuwara Eliya.

Evening

We will then continue our scenic drive to Nuwara Eliya, arriving at your hotel by the late afternoon. You will have the evening to rest and prepare for the next day's exploration of Sri Lanka's beautiful highlands.

Highlights: Divurumpola Temple (Ramayana Trail Site) Ravana Cave (Ramayana Trail Site)

Morning

Your day begins with a significant pilgrimage to the Divurumpola Temple in Welimada. This site is of immense importance on the Ramayana trail as it is believed to be the location of the 'Agni Pariksha', or the Trial by Fire, which Sita underwent to prove her chastity after her rescue. Even today, the temple is a place where sacred oaths are taken, as it is believed that any false vow made here will be exposed by the power of truth, a legacy of Sita's trial. Visiting this temple offers a deep sense of connection to the epic's most pivotal moments.

Afternoon

We will then travel to Ella, a scenic town famous for its natural beauty and legendary connections. Here, we'll embark on a short trek to the famous Ravana Cave. This cave is part of a fascinating network of natural and man-made tunnels that once served as a secret passage for King Ravana. According to local lore, this is where Ravana hid Sita, adding another layer of intrigue to the story. The hike itself is rewarding, leading to a stunning opening that reveals a breathtaking view of the surrounding landscape, providing a true sense of adventure.

Evening

After a fulfilling day of historical and spiritual exploration, we will return to Nuwara Eliya for a relaxing evening at your hotel. Dinner will be served, allowing you to reflect on the legends and beauty you have experienced.

Highlights: Kelaniya Temple (Ramayana Trail Site)

Morning

Enjoy a final, delicious breakfast at your hotel in Nuwara Eliya before checking out. We'll then begin our journey to Colombo, traveling from the cool, misty highlands to the warm, bustling cityscapes of the capital.

Afternoon

Along the way, we will stop for a lunch break (not included). Our next stop is the revered Kelaniya Temple, a site of immense spiritual and historical significance. In the context of the Ramayana, this is where Vibhishana, the righteous brother of King Ravana, was crowned as the new ruler of Lanka by Lord Rama. The temple is also believed to have been visited by Lord Buddha during his third and final visit to Sri Lanka, making it a sacred site for both Hindu and Buddhist pilgrims. We plan to arrive around 2:00 PM, giving you time to explore its beautiful architecture and profound history.

Evening

After our visit to Kelaniya Temple, we will proceed to your hotel in Colombo, arriving around 3:30 PM. You'll have the remainder of the afternoon and early evening to relax and prepare for your final dinner, which will be served at your hotel.

FAQ

Can we change the itinerary?

This is a pre-planned and optimized itinerary, but we can certainly create a custom plan for you if you have specific places you want to visit or changes you'd like to make. The current plan is designed for the best 6-day Ramayana experience in Sri Lanka.

Can we add more passengers to the tour?

Our reservations for hotels and vehicles are based on the number of people in your group to ensure a comfortable trip. If you need to add more guests, please contact us so we can check for any extra costs or modifications that may be needed.

Can we change the tour dates?

We make hotel and guide reservations as soon as full payment is received to secure your booking. Due to limited availability, date modifications cannot be guaranteed, but please let us know your situation and we will do our best to assist you.

Can we pay in installments?

To ensure we can secure your hotel and guide reservations in advance, full payment is required at the time of booking. Unfortunately, we are not able to support installment payments for this package.
